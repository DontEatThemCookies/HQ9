HHQQP

this is a sample input file for the interpreter.
valid operations: uppercase h, q, p and the number nine in digit form.
it is recommended to comment in lowercase and not use the number nine
in order to not interfere with the operations.

the specific operations in this example are:

HH - Execute HELLO WORLD 2 times 
QQ - Quine two "Q" characters (outputs QQQQ)
P - Increment the accumulator once
